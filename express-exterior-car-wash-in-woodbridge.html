Frequently Asked Questions About Express Exterior Car Wash

How long does an express exterior car wash take at Edge Express Car Wash?
Our express exterior car wash typically takes 3-5 minutes from start to finish. You'll drive through our automated tunnel system, and our high-efficiency equipment handles the washing, rinsing, and drying process quickly. During busy periods, especially weekends and after work hours, you might wait 5-10 minutes in line, but we've designed our system to keep things moving smoothly for Woodbridge commuters.
What's included in your exterior car wash packages and how much do they cost?
We offer three main packages: Basic Wash ($8) includes soap, rinse, and air dry; Premium Wash ($12) adds tire shine, undercarriage rinse, and spot-free rinse; and our Signature Wash ($16) includes everything plus rain repellent, wheel cleaner, and premium wax protection. All packages include our high-pressure pre-rinse to remove road salt and debris - especially important here in Northern Virginia during winter months.
Do you guarantee your car wash service, and what if I'm not satisfied?
Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our wash packages. If you're not completely happy with your wash, simply return within 24 hours with your receipt and we'll re-wash your vehicle at no charge. We also guarantee that our equipment won't damage your vehicle when used properly - our brushes are soft-cloth and regularly maintained to ensure safe cleaning for all vehicle types.
What should I do to prepare my car before going through the wash?
Before entering our wash tunnel, please remove or secure any loose items like antennas, bike racks, or trailer hitches. Close all windows, sunroofs, and convertible tops completely. Our staff will guide you onto the track and put your vehicle in neutral - just keep your hands off the steering wheel and brakes during the wash process. We recommend removing excessive mud or debris beforehand, which is common on vehicles around Woodbridge due to construction and rural roads in the area.
Are you open during bad weather, and do you wash cars in winter?
Yes, we're open year-round and operate in most weather conditions, including light rain and snow. Winter is actually one of our busiest seasons since Virginia roads use salt and brine treatments that can damage your vehicle's finish. Our heated facility and warm water system work effectively even in freezing temperatures. We only close during severe weather warnings or when ice makes our lot unsafe. During winter months, we especially recommend our undercarriage wash to remove road salt buildup.
